~ Our Story ~

Rooted in Tradition, Growing with Love

❋

Sri Lakshmi Farm Products was born from a simple belief: the best spices come from healthy soil, patient farmers, and time-honoured methods.

Based in Miryalaguda, Telangana — the heart of South India's agricultural belt — we grow, harvest, and process our spices ourselves. Every product on our shelf is a product of our own land and our own hands.

We believe that good food starts with good spices, and good spices start with the right soil. That's why we remain committed to natural farming, with no synthetic pesticides and no artificial enhancers.

🌍
Locally Grown in Telangana — Supporting regional farmers and keeping traditions alive in the Nalgonda district.
🧑‍🌾
Family-Run Business — Personal attention to quality at every step because our name is on every packet.
🫙
